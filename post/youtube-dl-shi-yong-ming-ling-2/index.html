youtube-dl使用命令2

· 2020-04-28 ·

1、查看分辨率：

youtube-dl -F --proxy socks5://127.0.0.1:7891/ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lhel0tzHE08

2、选择最清晰的MP4
youtube-dl -f best --proxy socks5://127.0.0.1:7891/ --write-sub --write-auto-sub --write-all-thumbnails https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nkNZ1B1OaeE

youtube-dl -f 'bestvideo[ext=mp4]+bestaudio[ext=m4a]/bestvideo+bestaudio' --proxy socks5://127.0.0.1:7891/ --write-sub --write-auto-sub --write-all-thumbnails --convert-subs vtt https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lhel0tzHE08

youtube-dl -f 'bestvideo[ext=mp4]+bestaudio[ext=m4a]/bestvideo+bestaudio' --proxy socks5://127.0.0.1:7891/ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lhel0tzHE08

--merge-output-format mp4
